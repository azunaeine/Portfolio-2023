Visual Design

Bold

Project Summary

The objective of this project was to conceptualize, refine, choose, and design a sign-in page that incorporates an adjective to establish a distinct personality. Furthermore, the project necessitated the application of visual design techniques to effectively convey the intended adjective without overtly stating it. The project progressed through stages of ideation, iterative refinement, careful selection, and meticulous design implementation. By leveraging visual design skills, the aim is to evoke the desired adjective in a subtle and compelling manner.

Final Design

Bold

The final design of the project incorporates a monochromatic yellow background, carefully chosen for its symbolic representation of intelligence and optimism. These qualities closely align with the concept of boldness, commonly observed in individuals who exhibit daring and audacious behavior. Additionally, the design features an eye element, symbolizing attributes of protection, insight, and wisdom. The gradual formation of the eye's inner layer symbolizes the progressive development of boldness, fueled by the acquisition of wisdom and deeper understanding. This visual representation establishes a strong connection between the eye element, wisdom, and the concept of boldness, effectively reinforcing the underlying theme throughout the entire design. It communicates that boldness is not solely driven by audacity, but also by the careful consideration and discernment that wisdom brings, creating a powerful and well-rounded embodiment of boldness.

Alternative sign-in pages

Magical

This sign-in page employs a restricted color palette, featuring two equally relevant hues of blue. Additionally, a black accent color is incorporated into the design. Contrast is utilized to convey the adjective "magical," particularly through the use of contrasting shapes. A simplistic shape is juxtaposed with a more intricate shape. However, after careful evaluation, I chose not to proceed with this sign-in page as the final design. I believe that the visual elements and colors fail to effectively capture the intended essence of "magical."

Rueful

The term "rueful" is synonymous with regret. The design of the sign-in page employs a black and white color palette, where black symbolizes grief and white represents innocence. The deliberate combination of these colors aims to effectively convey the essence of ruefulness. The design incorporates thick and long squares arranged in intersecting horizontal and vertical lines, creating a visually striking cage-like structure. However, the decision was made to not proceed with this sign-in page as the final design due to its perceived dullness and lack of appeal.

The planning stage

The planning stage holds significant importance as it grants the designer the necessary liberty to engage in experimentation. Prior to the development of three distinct sign-in pages, each representing a unique adjective, it was imperative to commence with sketching and creating wireframes. By undertaking these activities, a comprehensive comprehension of the visual aspects and overall appearance of the three sign-in pages was obtained.

Reflection

This school project was highly enjoyable and insightful. Upon reflection, I would advise my future self to embrace the opportunity to explore new avenues and broaden my expertise in the field of design. While I focused primarily on UX Design and honed my skills in that area, I recognized the significance of visual design as another critical aspect of design that requires further development.
