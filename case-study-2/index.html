UI/UX Design

MAZE

Summary

Tutoring services play a crucial role in assisting students in comprehending challenging concepts and addressing problems encountered in their school subjects. This mobile application integrates video calling, planning, and professional tutors within a single platform, providing a comprehensive tutoring experience.

Role

  • User Research,
  • Visual Design,
  • UX Design

Tools

  • Figma

Timeline

  • 10 weeks

Team

  • Azuna Ekundayo

Prototype

The Problem

Many students struggle to understand difficult concepts and complete their school projects and assignments, necessitating the need for effective tutoring services. However, existing tutoring solutions often lack an integrated and comprehensive approach. The absence of a mobile application that combines video calling, planning, and professional tutors limits students' access to convenient and personalized support. Therefore, there is a need for a tutoring platform that seamlessly integrates these functionalities to provide students with a holistic and efficient learning experience.

The Solution

My solution is to develop a mobile application that revolutionizes the tutoring experience by integrating video calling, planning, and access to professional tutors. By leveraging the power of technology, my platform will provide students with a convenient and personalized learning environment. Through seamless communication via video calls, students can engage in real-time interactive sessions with experienced tutors who can address their specific needs and challenges. The application will also offer planning features to help students organize their assignment deadlines. By combining these essential components, my solution aims to enhance students' understanding of difficult concepts and empower them to excel in their school subjects.

Video Calls

The platform facilitates seamless video calling functionality, allowing users to connect and engage in real-time video sessions with their preferred tutors.

Planning

The planning feature enables users to effectively organize assignment deadlines, providing them with the ability to manage their academic tasks efficiently.

Tracker

Users are provided with the functionality to monitor and track the progress of their lessons in collaboration with their tutors.

Understanding the problem

After reflecting on the problem statement, I wanted to dive deeper into what I was solving so I began to ask myself a series of questions.

  • What are the needs and goals of the users?
  • Why is this problem impactful and significant?
  • How can the app be effectively designed to accommodate all user needs and requirements?

Feature Prioritization

​​A visual graph was created to outline and prioritize potential features for the mobile application. This graph serves as a valuable reference for identifying focal points, strategizing future enhancements, and determining which features will be incorporated into the current version of the mobile application.

Affinity Mapping

Based on the data extracted from the graph, I analyzed and organized the pertinent attributes into distinct categories.

Defining the Grid System

The selection and implementation of a grid system is a fundamental aspect of interface design. In this project, I opted for the 6-point grid system due to its adaptability and suitability. I made a concerted effort to align UI elements with the grid whenever feasible.

Wireframes

Wireframing is an essential process in my project as it allows me to visualize and plan the layout and structure of the user interface. By creating wireframes, I am able to map out the placement of key elements, such as buttons, menus, and content sections, ensuring a clear and intuitive design. These wireframes serve as a blueprint for the development phase, providing a solid foundation for building an effective and user-friendly interface.

High Fidelity Design

Upon completion of the wireframing process, I gained a clear vision of the final concept for the mobile application. Subsequently, I proceeded to implement various design elements, such as colors, icons, and other visual components, onto MAZE. These additions further enhanced the aesthetic appeal and user experience of the application, aligning it with the intended design direction.

Reflection

This project necessitated a spirit of experimentation as it involved venturing into uncharted territory. Reflecting upon the experience, I would advise myself to cultivate greater confidence in my design skills and to embrace a mindset of perseverance and continual experimentation.
