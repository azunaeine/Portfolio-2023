About Me

My name is Azuna Ekundayo, and I am currently enrolled at Sheridan College, pursuing a major in Interaction Design. Originally from Nigeria, I was raised in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada, and now I reside in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. I possess a deep passion for UX design and web design, driven by my desire to create intuitive solutions and visually appealing products. With extensive internship experience and a profound understanding of UX Design and web design, I offer valuable expertise in these domains. Should you wish to contact me, please feel free to reach out via email or phone. Outside of designing, you can find me engrossed in the latest TV shows, indulging in a good book, or engaging in coding activities. I have developed my portfolio using Adobe XD for the design phase and implemented it using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the coding aspect. I hope you find my portfolio enjoyable and informative.
